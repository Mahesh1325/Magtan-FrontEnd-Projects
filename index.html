How It Works

01

Prepare & Shower

Arrive 15 minutes early. We provide everything you need—earplugs, towels, and body wash inside your private suite.

02

Step In & Let Go

Step into the skin-temperature water. Close the lid (or leave it open) and turn off the light when you're ready.

03

Awaken & Integrate

Gentle music will signal the end of your session. Shower, dress, and enjoy organic tea in our integration lounge.

Quick Answers

Common questions before your first float.

Is it safe if I can't swim?

Absolutely. The water in our pods is only 25–30 cm deep. The salt concentration makes you float effortlessly — no swimming required.

What should I bring?

Nothing — we provide towels, earplugs, and premium organic toiletries. Just bring yourself. Avoid caffeine 4–6 hours before your session.

Can I float if pregnant?

Many pregnant guests love floating, especially in the second trimester. Always consult your physician first. We're happy to discuss positioning accommodations.

How clean is the water?

Our pods are fully filtered and treated between every session using UV, ozone, and hydrogen peroxide. The high salinity is naturally antimicrobial.

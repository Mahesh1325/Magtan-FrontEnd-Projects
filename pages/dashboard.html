Pre-Float Instructions

Before Your Session

  • Avoid shaving or waxing 12 hours before floating
  • Don't drink caffeine 3 hours before your session
  • Eat a light meal 1–2 hours beforehand
  • Remove contact lenses before entering the pod
  • Arrive 15 minutes early to complete check-in

During Your Float

  • Shower before entering the pod (soap provided)
  • Insert provided earplugs to keep water out
  • You control the lid and light — open or closed, your choice
  • If Epsom salt gets in your eyes, use the spray bottle provided
  • Gentle music signals the end of your session

After Your Float

  • Shower again to rinse off the salt water
  • Take your time in the integration lounge — don't rush
  • Drink water and enjoy the herbal teas provided
  • Journal your experience if you'd like — journals available

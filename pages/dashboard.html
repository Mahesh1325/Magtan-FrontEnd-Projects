Common Questions

❓ Can I float if I'm pregnant?

Yes! Floating is wonderful for pregnant clients. We have maternity pods and our staff is trained to assist. Please mention this when booking.

❓ Will my hair get damaged by salt water?

Our Epsom salt is gentler than ocean salt. Rinse thoroughly after to prevent drying. Consider wearing a shower cap if you have treated hair.

❓ Can I bring earbuds or music?

We recommend floating in silence to maximize benefits, but some pods support audio if you prefer gentle music.

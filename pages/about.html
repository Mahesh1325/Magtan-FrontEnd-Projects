About FLOAT.

A sanctuary crafted for ultimate disconnect.

Our Philosophy

In a world of constant notification, screen time, and hyper-connectivity, absolute silence has become the ultimate luxury. FLOAT was founded on the belief that giving our nervous system a complete rest is essential for modern health.

Our facility was architecturally designed from the ground up to support this mission. From acoustic isolation to custom lighting design, every detail aims to seamlessly guide you into and out of your float experience.

The Space

Clinical-Grade Pods

We use state-of-the-art sensory deprivation pods featuring dual-filtration systems, UV light purification, and ozone sanitation between every single float.

Private Suites

Each float pod sits within a sound-proofed private suite. You have your own rain shower, premium organic toiletries, and changing area.

Integration Lounge

Post-float, unwind in our post-float lounge. Journal, drink herbal tea, and slowly transition back into the world.

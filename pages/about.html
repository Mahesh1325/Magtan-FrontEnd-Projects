Who We Are

About FLOAT

A purpose-built sanctuary crafted for ultimate physical recovery and mental disconnection.

Our Philosophy

In a world of constant notification, screen time, and hyper-connectivity, absolute silence has become the ultimate luxury. FLOAT was founded on the belief that giving our nervous system a complete rest is essential for modern health.

Our facility was architecturally designed from the ground up to support this mission. From acoustic isolation to custom lighting design, every detail aims to seamlessly guide you into and out of your float experience.

Origin Story

The Haus Journey

FloatHaus began in 2018 as a single pod in a converted garage, born out of a desperate need to find quiet in a rapidly accelerating world. Our founders realized that the profound states of calm they sought were inaccessible to most people living demanding, urban lives.

They spent three years researching sensory science, consulting with neuroscientists, architects, and hydrotherapy experts to engineer the perfect environment. The result is what you see today: a facility where light, sound, and temperature are masterfully controlled to strip away external stimuli.

Today, FloatHaus serves over a thousand members monthly. We believe that by providing a sanctuary of nothingness, we empower our community to achieve everything.

The Space

Our facility was designed from the inside out — every material, surface, and system exists to deepen your rest.

Clinical-Grade Pods

State-of-the-art sensory deprivation pods with dual-filtration systems, UV light purification, and ozone sanitation between every session — exceeding industry hygiene standards.

Private Suites

Each pod lives inside a sound-proofed private suite with a rain shower, premium organic toiletries, a changing area, and personal climate control.

Integration Lounge

Post-float, transition gently back to reality in our calm lounge. Journal, sip herbal tea, and let the clarity of your float settle while the world waits.

Acoustic Isolation

Triple-layer acoustic dampening separates each suite. From the moment you enter, outside noise is a memory. Only the sound of your own breath remains.

Epsom Salt Solution

1,000 lbs of pharmaceutical-grade magnesium sulfate in each pod, creating a buoyancy that makes floating completely effortless — even for first-timers.

Skin-Temperature Water

Water and air are maintained at precise skin temperature (34.1°C). The goal: remove all external data so your brain can stop processing the physical world.

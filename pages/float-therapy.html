What is Float Therapy?

Frequently Asked Questions

What if I'm claustrophobic?

You are always in control. You can leave the pod door fully open, partially open, or keep the interior lights on over the duration of your float.

Do I need to bring anything?

Just yourself! We provide towels, earplugs, body wash, shampoo, and a prep area with blow dryers. Floating is done in the nude to eliminate tactile sensation.

Is the water clean?

Absolutely. The high salt content makes it incredibly sterile, but we also filter the water 3-4 times between each float through a 1-micron filter, UV light, and Ozone purification.
